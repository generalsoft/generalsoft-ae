IoT Security

Comprehensive security measures to protect IoT deployments:

  • Encrypted Communication: TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit

  • Access Control: Role-based access and fine-grained permissions
  • Threat Detection: Anomaly detection and intrusion prevention systems

Our Development Process

We follow a systematic approach for IoT and embedded systems projects:

  • Requirements Analysis: Define use cases, sensor requirements, connectivity needs, and power constraints
  • Hardware Selection: Choose optimal microcontrollers, sensors, and communication modules
  • Prototype Development: Build proof-of-concept with evaluation boards and breadboards
  • Firmware Development: Develop robust, tested firmware with proper error handling
  • Cloud Integration: Connect devices to cloud platforms for data storage and analytics
  • Testing & Validation: Rigorous testing including environmental, stress, and security testing
  • Production Support: Manufacturing support, quality assurance, and ongoing maintenance
